Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download the PopSide app, tap "Sign Up," and follow the onboarding process. You'll need to provide your name, email, city, and complete verification steps for community safety.
Is PopSide free to use?
Yes! PopSide is completely free to use. Create activities, join meetups, message other dads, and build community at no cost.
Why do I need to verify my account?
Safety is our top priority. Verification (email, phone, social media) helps ensure all members are real people, creating a trustworthy community for dads and their kids.
How do I join an activity?
Browse activities on the Home or Explore screen, tap one that interests you, and press "Join Activity." You'll be added to the participant list and can access the group chat.
Can I create my own activities?
Absolutely! Tap the "+" button in the center of the bottom navigation to create an activity. Choose the type, location, date/time, and add details. Other dads in your area will see it and can join.
How do I report inappropriate behavior?
Tap the three dots menu on any user's profile or activity, then select "Report." You can also block users directly. For urgent safety concerns, email popside.contact@gmail.com.
Can I use PopSide if I don't live in Ontario?
Currently, PopSide is focused on Ontario communities (Hamilton, Toronto, Burlington, Oakville, Mississauga, Kitchener-Waterloo, Ottawa). We plan to expand to other provinces in the future!
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account." Your profile and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
I found a bug or have a feature suggestion
We'd love to hear from you! Email popside.contact@gmail.com with details about the bug or your idea. Your feedback helps make PopSide better for everyone.
